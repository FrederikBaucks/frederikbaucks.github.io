Publications

Welcome to my portfolio of publications, where advanced data analytics and machine learning intersect with educational strategies. Here, you'll find my research on using Item Response Theory to assess course difficulty variations, contributing to enhanced curriculum design. Among the featured works is the development of a dashboard tool for academic counseling, which serves as a bridge between theoretical research and practical application. Highlighted as well is my work mitigating biases for curriculum analytics measures with an additive grade point model.
